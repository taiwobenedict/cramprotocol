Privacy and Direct transactions

Instead of relying on centralized authentication systems, Cram browser incorporate blockchain-based decentralized identity solutions. Users have control over their identities, reducing the risk of data breaches and enhancing privacy. Integrate cryptocurrency wallets, This facilitates micropayments for content creators and service providers, fostering a more direct and efficient economy.

DAO

Participate in decision-making processes regarding the development and governance of the browser. Supports a more democratic and community-driven model.

Resilience to DDoS Attacks

By leveraging decentralized networks, web3 browsers are less susceptible to Distributed Denial of Service (DDoS) attacks. The distributed nature of the infrastructure makes it challenging for attackers to overwhelm a single point of access.

VPN Introduction

VPNs can protect users from network surveillance and monitoring, which is particularly important in decentralized systems where privacy and security are paramount.
